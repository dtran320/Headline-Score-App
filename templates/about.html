Introduction

During my fellowship at Insight Data Science I took on a three week consulting project for PRX, a public relations startup. Prior to Insight I was working at a biotech startup working on automating a blood test to help doctors better personalize cancer prevention and treatment options. In parallel, I was doing a computational biology postdoc at the Lawrence Berkeley Lab, using bioinformatics tools and computational models to study the biological effects of different types of ionizing radiation. This was my first foray into natural language processing (NLP), which turned out to be a blast!


PRX is a startup that wants to make public relations easy and more transparent for their customers.
